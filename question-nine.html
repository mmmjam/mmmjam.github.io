What are the submission guidelines?

Submitting a solution writeup is a required component of the 2022 math jam.

Standard Formatting: final solutions must use the Times New Roman or a similar font, and use size 12 font for text that is not headings, titles, or other forms of formatting. Students are welcome to choose how to organize the sections of their solution and whether to include color.

In your paper, please include a title, problem statement, assumptions, a walkthrough of your model, your final solution, and a conclusion. There are templates included in the problem documents that you can use to help build your paper. Students participating in the senior division must also include references at the end of the paper using MLA formatting.
