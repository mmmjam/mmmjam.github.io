What is M3J?

M3J is a free math modeling experience hosted over Zoom from June 6th to June 12th. We're a part of TouchTomorrow, and will be hosting workshops all week.

Do I need prior experience with math modeling?

Not at all! We have resources to help you learn along the way.

Why should I do M3J?

You'll get to learn about some of the cool ways math modeling can be used in the real world!
